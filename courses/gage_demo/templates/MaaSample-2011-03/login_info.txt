<h2>MAA WeBWorK DEMO Course</h2>

This is a sample course you can use to experiment with WeBWorK.

<p>
To login, use one of the login names and its password
on the card you were given for this workshop.
(Alternatively, click on the <strong>Guest Login</strong> button
to view problems without supplying a password.)

<p>
If you login as a student (<strong>jdoe</strong> or <strong>jsmith</strong>),
you will see the student interface and can work as a student.

<p>
If you login as a professor (<strong>profa</strong> or <strong>profb</strong>),
you will see the instructor interface and can work as an instructor.
